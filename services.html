Environmental & Social Impact Assessment (ESIA)

Our flagship service — rigorous, multidisciplinary ESIAs that satisfy the requirements of Mozambican environmental law (Lei n.º 20/97 and Decreto n.º 54/2015) and the environmental and social safeguard policies of international development banks.

  • Preparation of Instruction Process for environmental categorisation
  • Pre-feasibility studies and Environmental Scoping Reports
  • Terms of Reference (ToR) preparation and regulatory submission
  • Baseline environmental and socioeconomic surveys
  • Community and stakeholder public consultation meetings
  • Impact identification, prediction, and significance evaluation
  • Preparation of the final ESIA Report including ESMP
  • Follow-up support during regulatory review
World Bank OP 4.01 AfDB Standards JICA Guidelines EU EIA Directive

Environmental & Social Management Plans (ESMP/EMP)

Practical, implementable management plans that translate ESIA findings into actionable mitigation measures, monitoring programmes, and institutional arrangements for the project's lifecycle.

  • Mitigation measures for all identified environmental and social impacts
  • Environmental and social monitoring frameworks with KPIs
  • Roles, responsibilities, and institutional capacity assessment
  • Budget estimates for ESMP implementation
  • Grievance Redress Mechanisms (GRM)
  • Gender-Based Violence (GBV) and Sexual Exploitation & Abuse (SEA) prevention mechanisms
  • Health, safety, and occupational risk management protocols
  • Decommissioning and site closure plans

Environmental Audits

Independent, systematic assessments of a project's or facility's environmental compliance status against national legislation, licence conditions, and international standards. Our certified Environmental Auditor (MTA, 2018) leads all audit engagements.

  • Compliance audits against ESMP/EMP requirements
  • Regulatory compliance audits (MITADER/ANAMA requirements)
  • Environmental due diligence for transactions and financing
  • Site inspections and decommissioning audits
  • Preparation of Audit Reports with corrective action plans
  • Post-audit follow-up and verification

Resettlement Action Plans (RAP / ARAP)

Culturally sensitive and legally compliant resettlement planning in accordance with World Bank OP 4.12 and Mozambican land law, ensuring fair treatment and livelihood restoration for affected communities.

  • Census and socioeconomic baseline surveys of affected populations
  • Asset inventory and valuation
  • Eligibility and entitlement framework development
  • Abbreviated Resettlement Action Plans (ARAP) for minor displacement
  • Full Resettlement Action Plans (RAP) for significant displacement
  • Livelihood restoration programmes
  • Community consultation and participation throughout
  • GRM design and implementation support

Environmental Supervision & Monitoring

On-site environmental supervision during construction and operational phases to verify ESMP implementation, identify non-conformances, and ensure continuous compliance with environmental licence conditions.

  • Regular site inspections and environmental performance monitoring
  • Health, safety & environmental (EHS) supervision and reporting
  • Non-conformance tracking and corrective action follow-up
  • Environmental inspection checklists and field data collection
  • Preparation of periodic environmental monitoring reports
  • Air, water, noise, and soil quality monitoring
  • Borrow pit, quarry, and camp site environmental management
  • Decommissioning inspection and site closure verification

Training & Capacity Building

Tailored training programmes for government agencies, project developers, contractors, and communities to build lasting environmental management capacity at all levels.

  • ESIA methodology and Mozambican EIA regulations
  • Environmental safeguards for World Bank and AfDB-financed projects
  • EHS management systems for construction contractors
  • Community environmental awareness sessions
  • Grievance mechanism facilitation and community liaison training
  • GBV prevention and response training
  • Environmental monitoring and data collection techniques
