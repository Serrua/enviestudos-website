Who We Are

18+ Years Serving Mozambique's Development

Founded in June 2011, Enviestudos, Lda. (EEL) brings together multidisciplinary professionals with deep knowledge of Mozambique's environmental, social, and regulatory landscape.

  • International standard compliance — all studies align with WB, AfDB, JICA, EU safeguard policies.

  • National reach — projects executed in all 10 provinces of Mozambique.

  • Certified expertise — Ministry of Land & Environment certified consultants and auditors.

  • Pragmatic approach — solutions that are technically sound, locally relevant, and implementable.
