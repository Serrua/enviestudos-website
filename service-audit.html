Environmental Audits & Compliance

Independent, systematic assessments of environmental compliance led by a certified Environmental Auditor (MTA, 2018).

Home› Services› Audits

What We Do

We deliver independent environmental audits that objectively measure a project’s compliance against its Environmental and Social Management Plan (ESMP), national licence conditions, and applicable international standards. Our lead auditor holds a certified Environmental Auditor qualification from the Mozambican Technical Authority (MTA, 2018).

  • Compliance audits against ESMP/EMP requirements and licence conditions
  • Regulatory compliance audits per MITADER/ANAMA requirements
  • Environmental due diligence for transactions, financing, and acquisitions
  • Site inspections and decommissioning audits
  • Preparation of Audit Reports with corrective action plans
  • Post-audit follow-up and corrective action verification
  • Performance audits assessing environmental management effectiveness

Types of Audits

A

ESMP Compliance Audit

Verifies whether the contractor and project operator are implementing the Environmental and Social Management Plan as approved by regulators and funders.

B

Regulatory Compliance Audit

Evaluates compliance with the Ambiental Licence conditions, applicable Mozambican legislation, and any funder-specific environmental covenants.

C

Environmental Due Diligence

Structured assessment of environmental liabilities, risks, and compliance gaps for project finance transactions, mergers, acquisitions, or asset transfers.

D

Decommissioning Audit

End-of-project audit verifying that all site closure requirements have been met — camps, quarries, borrow pits, access routes, and spoil areas.

Audit Methodology

01

Document Review

Review of approved ESMP, environmental licence, monitoring reports, contractor records, and previous audit findings.

02

Site Inspection

Structured field inspection using audit checklists covering all ESMP provisions, including waste management, water protection, erosion control, and community relations.

03

Stakeholder Interviews

Structured interviews with project management, environmental officers, workers, and affected community representatives.

04

Gap Analysis & Corrective Action Plan

Identification of non-conformances, root-cause analysis, and a prioritised corrective action plan with responsibilities and timelines.
