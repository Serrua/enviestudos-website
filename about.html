Our Story

Born from Expertise, Built on Trust

Enviestudos, Lda. (EEL) was constituted on the 9th of June 2011 in Maputo, Mozambique, in the light of legislation in force in the country, by individual consultants with extensive professional experience in the national market.

The Company was founded to enhance the synergies associated with its founders' combined work experience in environmental impact assessment studies, management, advisory, marketing, and professional training — with the goal of positioning itself as one of the largest environmental consultancy firms in Mozambique.

Today, Enviestudos has a multidisciplinary team with recognised experience acquired over more than 18 years in the Mozambican market, having participated in ESIAs across mining, tourism, agriculture, energy, roads, and infrastructure sectors, financed by the World Bank, AfDB, JICA, EU, and more.

Our Experts

Meet the Leadership Team

Our founders bring decades of combined expertise in environmental science, geology, and international project management.

AP
Technical Director

Arcangelo Passela

BSc in Environmental Geology from Eduardo Mondlane University (1996), specialisation in ESIA at Aveiro University, Portugal (1998), and candidate for a Master in Engineering & Environmental Technology at Universidad Europea del Atlántico, Spain.

With over 25 years of experience, Mr. Passela is one of Mozambique's first certified Environmental Consultants (MTA, 2002) and a certified Environmental Auditor (MTA, 2018). He has led ESIAs across mining, tourism, agriculture, energy, roads and infrastructure sectors for projects financed by the World Bank, AfDB, JICA, and EU.

BSc Environmental Geology Certified Env. Consultant — MTA Certified Env. Auditor — MTA 25+ Years Experience
AG
General Manager / Partner

Amad Gani

BSc in Geology from Eduardo Mondlane University (2001), Post-Graduate in Socioeconomics of Development (ISCTEM Maputo, 2011), and MBA in Oil & Gas (ISCTEM Maputo, 2016).
