Environmental Services

Environmental Supervision & Monitoring

On-site supervision during construction and operations to verify ESMP implementation, identify non-conformances, and ensure continuous environmental compliance.

Home› Services› Supervision

What We Do

Effective ESMP implementation requires experienced, independent oversight on the ground. Our environmental supervisors are embedded in project teams — tracking performance, identifying problems early, and ensuring that environmental and social commitments translate into real outcomes at the worksite.

  • Regular site inspections and environmental performance monitoring
  • Health, Safety & Environment (EHS) supervision and reporting
  • Non-conformance tracking and corrective action follow-up
  • Environmental inspection checklists and field data collection
  • Preparation of periodic environmental monitoring reports
  • Air, water, noise, and soil quality monitoring
  • Borrow pit, quarry, and camp site environmental management
  • Decommissioning inspection and site closure verification
  • Contractor EHS capacity building and toolbox talks

Our Process

01

Mobilisation & Planning

Review of approved ESMP, environmental licence, and project drawings. Preparation of site-specific environmental management plans, inspection checklists, and monitoring schedules.

02

Field Supervision

Regular site visits — daily during critical activities, weekly otherwise. Coverage includes all active work fronts: earthworks, stream crossings, material stockpiles, camps, and access roads.

03

Compliance Monitoring

Systematic monitoring against ESMP provisions, environmental licence conditions, and funder requirements. Data recorded using standardised field forms and photographic evidence.

04

Non-Conformance Management

Non-conformances logged, root causes identified, corrective actions issued to contractor with deadlines, and close-out verified at follow-up inspections.

05

Reporting

Monthly and quarterly environmental monitoring reports submitted to project owner, funder, and regulator. Reports include performance indicators, compliance status, and corrective action tracking.

06

Site Closure Verification

Final decommissioning inspection of all sites: borrow pits, quarries, camp sites, access routes, and spoil areas. Preparation of decommissioning report for regulatory sign-off.
